### 📅 2026

**⭐ Curated picks**

- [`🎓 Training`](README.md#3--training-methods) · Advancing Spatiotemporal Representations in SNNs via Parametric Invertible Transformation (PIT) (**ICLR 2026**). \[[paper](https://openreview.net/forum?id=3JwNXQzxll)\]\[[code](https://github.com/YinsongYan/ICLR26)\]
