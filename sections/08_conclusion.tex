\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

% Summary of the survey; the promise of SNNs for energy-efficient, brain-inspired
% computing; outlook.

Spiking Neural Networks represent a promising path toward energy-efficient,
brain-inspired computation. As training methods mature and neuromorphic
hardware becomes more capable, SNNs are poised to complement conventional deep
learning in latency- and power-constrained settings. Continued progress will
depend on advances in scalable training, standardized benchmarks, and tight
hardware-software co-design.
