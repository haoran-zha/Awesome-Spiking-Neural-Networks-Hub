\section{Challenges and Future Directions}
\label{sec:challenges}

% Accuracy gap vs. ANNs; scalable training; standardized benchmarks and datasets;
% hardware-software co-design; theory of temporal computation; large-scale SNNs and
% spiking transformers.

\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item \textbf{Training scalability:} efficient learning for deep and large SNNs.
  \item \textbf{Benchmarks:} lack of standardized, event-native datasets and metrics.
  \item \textbf{Hardware-software co-design:} bridging algorithms and neuromorphic chips.
  \item \textbf{Theory:} understanding the role of temporal dynamics in computation.
  \item \textbf{Emerging architectures:} spiking transformers and large-scale models.
\end{itemize}
