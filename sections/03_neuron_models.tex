\subsection{Integrate-and-Fire Family}
The Leaky Integrate-and-Fire (LIF) neuron models the membrane potential $V(t)$ as
\begin{equation}
  \tau_m \frac{dV(t)}{dt} = -\big(V(t) - V_{\text{rest}}\big) + R\, I(t),
  \label{eq:lif}
\end{equation}
emitting a spike and resetting when $V(t)$ crosses a threshold $V_{\text{th}}$.
