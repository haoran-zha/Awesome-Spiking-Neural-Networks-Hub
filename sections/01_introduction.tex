\section{Introduction}
\label{sec:introduction}

% Motivation: why SNNs, energy efficiency, biological plausibility, neuromorphic computing.
% Contrast with conventional ANNs.
% Contributions and organization of this survey.

Deep Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs) have achieved remarkable success across
domains, yet their energy consumption and reliance on dense, synchronous
matrix operations contrast sharply with the efficiency of the biological
brain. Spiking Neural Networks (SNNs) offer an alternative computational
paradigm based on discrete, event-driven spikes and rich temporal dynamics.

\paragraph{Contributions.} This survey:
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=*]
  \item reviews the biological foundations and core neuron models of SNNs;
  \item categorizes and compares training methodologies;
  \item summarizes neuromorphic hardware platforms;
  \item surveys application domains and identifies open challenges.
\end{itemize}

\paragraph{Organization.} The remainder of this survey is organized as follows.
\Cref{sec:background} introduces background concepts;
\Cref{sec:neuron_models} discusses neuron models;
\Cref{sec:training} covers training methods;
\Cref{sec:hardware} reviews hardware;
\Cref{sec:applications} presents applications;
\Cref{sec:challenges} outlines challenges; and
\Cref{sec:conclusion} concludes.
